Hey there!

I’m Dylan. ✌️

my interest in photography started when I was 5—my parents bought me a VTech “toy” 1.3 megapixel camera. I took this camera everywhere with me. While the pictures were nothing close to virtuosic, they sparked a lifelong interest in capturing priceless moments with cameras.

When I was 14, I taught myself the art of developing film. Local lab prices were far too high to send film off at the rate I was taking photos, so I bought the chemicals online and I was hooked from there. I now own 9 cameras—from full-frame DSLRs to medium-format film cameras; I love taking photos!

Aside from loving photography, I am currently in my junior year of pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Statistics at Texas A&M University in College Station.
